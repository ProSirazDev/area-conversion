About This Tool

This area conversion tool is designed for converting between standard units (like Square Meters, Acres, and Hectares) and local units commonly used in West Bengal, India. Local units such as Bigha, Katha, Chitak, Satak, and Decimal have specific meanings in the context of land measurement.

  • 1 Bigha ≈ 1,338 square meters
  • 1 Katha ≈ 67 square meters
  • 1 Chitak ≈ 13.38 square meters
  • 1 Satak ≈ 40.47 square meters
  • 1 Decimal ≈ 40.47 square meters

These measurements are widely used for land transactions and agricultural planning in West Bengal. This tool provides easy conversions between these units and standard area units.
